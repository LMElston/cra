Line 31600 – Disability amount for self

Claiming the Disability tax credit

If you are approved for the Disability tax credit (DTC), you may be able to claim the disability amount of $8,576 on line 31600 of your tax return (Step 5 – Federal tax).

Tax tip: If you or anyone else paid for attendant care or for care in an establishment, special rules may apply.

For details: Attendant care or care in an establishment

Supplement for persons under 18 years

You can claim up to an additional $5,003 if you qualify for the disability amount and were under 18 years old at the end of the year.

This supplement may be reduced in 2020 if one of the following applies:

  • Someone claimed child care expenses (line 21400) or attendant care expenses line 33099 or 33199 for you on their tax return
  • You claimed attendant care expenses (line 21500 or line 33099) on your tax return

Complete the chart for line 31600 on the Worksheet for the return to calculate your claim.
