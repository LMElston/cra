  • Yes

    Q2 Has the impairment lasted or is it expected to last for a continuous period of at least 12 months?

    • Yes

      Q3 Is the impairment present all or substantially all of the time (at least 90% of the time)?

      • Yes

        Q4 Do you receive life-sustaining therapy that supports a vital function?

        This may include the following therapies:
        ○ kidney dialysis
        ○ insulin therapy
        ○ oxygen therapy
        ○ chest physiotherapy

        • Yes

          Q5 Do you receive the life-sustaining therapy at least 3 times per week, for an average of 14 hours per week?

          • Yes

            Q6 Are you blind in both eyes?

            One of the following must apply:
            ○ Your eyesight is 20/200 (6/60) or worse on an eye chart (Snellen or an equivalent) in both eyes
            ○ Your field of vision is less than 20 degrees in both eyes after correction

            • Yes
            • No

              Q7 Do you have severe or profound hearing loss in both ears even when using hearing support devices?

              Severe hearing loss is in the range of 71 to 90dB. Profound hearing loss is 91dB+.

              • Yes
              • No

                Q8 Do any of the following statements apply to you?

                ○ I am confined to a bed or wheelchair at all times.
                ○ I have had a limb amputated.
                ○ Tube feeding is my primary means of feeding myself.
                ○ I have chronic renal failure.
                ○ I require catheterization to manage my bladder functions.
                ○ Sign language is my primary means of communication.

                • Yes
                • No

                  Q9 Do you have an impairment in any of the following categories?

                  ○ vision

                  ○ eliminating (bowel or bladder functions)
                  ○ feeding (including food preparation)
                  ○ dressing
                  ○ mental functions necessary for everyday life

                  • Yes

                    This must be the case even with the appropriate medication, devices, and therapy.

                    • No

                        These limitations must exist together all or substantially all of the time.

                        ○ feeding (including food preparation)
                        ○ dressing
                        ○ mental functions necessary for everyday life
