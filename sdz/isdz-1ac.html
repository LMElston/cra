About Secure Drop Zone

The Drop Zone Management is an application for CRA users to manage one time, adhoc communications with external entities to either receive, or deliver documents.

Creating a Drop Zone will enable you to receive/deliver documents from/to an external user.

My Drop Zones will enable you to view all the Drop Zones that you have created.
