Claim the SR&ED investment tax credit

Learn how to claim the Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) investment tax credit, including information on linking your eligible work to the expenditures you can claim, grouping your work into SR&ED projects, and separating your eligible work from your other business activities.
